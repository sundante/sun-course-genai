# 06 — Agentic AI

## What Is Agentic AI

Agentic AI refers to systems designed to pursue goals autonomously over extended horizons — combining planning, memory, tool use, and multi-agent coordination to solve complex, open-ended tasks.

## Scope of This Section

This section focuses on the *system-level* view: architectural patterns, design principles, and multi-agent coordination. For framework-specific implementations, see [05 — Agents](../05-Agents/INDEX.md).
